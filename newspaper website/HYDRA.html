Chief Minister Sri A. Revanth Reddy ordered the officials to constitute the HYDRA (Hyderabad Disaster Response and Assets Monitoring and Protection) aiming to extend a wide range of services to the citizens in accordance with the fast expansion of the Hyderabad city.
