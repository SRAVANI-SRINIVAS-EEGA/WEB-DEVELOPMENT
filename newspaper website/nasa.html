SPACEX launches mission to return stranded NASA astronauts

SpaceXlaunched a rescue mission on Saturday to retrieve two astronauts, Butch Wilmore and Sunita Williams, who have been stuck at the International Space Station (ISS) after safety concerns grounded their Boeing spacecraft. The mission involves a downsized SpaceX crew sent to bring them home, although their return isn't expected until early next year. The SpaceX capsule rocketed toward orbit to retrieve Wilmore and Williams, after Boeing's Starliner spacecraft returned to Earth empty earlier this month due to a series of technical issues, including thruster problems and helium leaks.
