Our Choreographers

Classical

Martha Graham

Pioneered modern dance and developed a unique style that expresses deep emotions and human experiences.

Mikhail Baryshnikov

Regarded as one of the greatest ballet dancers, known for his exceptional technique and artistic expressiveness.

George Balanchine

A major figure in 20th-century ballet, he founded the New York City Ballet and created numerous iconic works.

Ballet

Anna Pavlova

One of the most celebrated ballerinas of the early 20th century, known for her role in "The Dying Swan."

Rudolf Nureyev

A legendary ballet dancer and choreographer, known for his passionate performances and collaborations.

Catherine de Braganza

A key figure in the establishment of ballet in England, known for her elegance and influence.

Belly Dance

Jillina

An internationally acclaimed dancer and choreographer, known for her innovative approach to belly dance.

Maha El Moussa

Famous for her performances and teaching in the Middle Eastern dance community, focusing on cultural authenticity.

Suhaila Salimpour

Known for her comprehensive method of belly dance training, blending traditional and modern techniques.

Hip-Hop

Kenrick Sandy

A prominent figure in hip-hop dance, known for his work with the UK-based group Boy Blue Entertainment.

Poppin' John

Renowned for his popping dance style and performances, he has gained international recognition.

Jabbawockeez

An iconic dance crew known for their unique style and innovative choreography in hip-hop dance.
